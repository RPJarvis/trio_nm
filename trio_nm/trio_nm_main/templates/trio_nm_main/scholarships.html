Hello TRIO NM, The TRIO NM Scholarship Committee is pleased to announce the release of the 2016-2017 TRIO NM Pre-Collegiate Scholarship. The application is attached and will be posted on the website. Please see the application for full details. More information about the scholarship is below: The TRIO New Mexico Pre-Collegiate Scholarship Application is for those TRIO students who are currently participating in a Talent Search, Upward Bound, Upward Bound Math/Science, Veteran's Upward Bound, or Educational Opportunity Program who will enroll at a post-secondary institution for Fall 2016. Students must be enrolled at a post-secondary institution for Fall 2016; the actual award of the scholarship will depend on verification of this enrollment. Fall scholarships will be awarded in September/October 2016. The complete application should be submitted as a single PDF document and must reach the Scholarship Committee by 5:00 p.m. MDT on Monday, July 25, 2016. Please send all materials as a single PDF emailed to: Erin Weddington, Scholarship Chair, TRIO Upward Bound Email: emwedd@unm.edu Phone: 505-277-3516 Best, TRIO NM Scholarship Committee Erin Weddington Instructor TRIO Upward Bound Program University of New Mexico Office: (505) 277-3516
